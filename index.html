Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe, is a landlocked country located in Southern Africa. It is home to one the the world's most breathtaking and beautiful natural wonders - Victoria Falls. Located on the border of Zimbabwe and Zambia, Victoria Falls is a reminder of the majestic power and beauty to be found in Africa. The mighty Zambezi River plunges over a 108-meter high cliff creating an unforgettable experience of thunder and mist.
