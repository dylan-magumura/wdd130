Our History

Founded in 2010, Splash White Water Rafting has been thrilling adventure-seekers with exhilarating white water rafting experiences. Our journey began with a deep passion and love for the mighty Zambezi River and Victoria Falls. We also have a deep commitment to safety and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we have honed our skills and perfected our craft, to become the best rafting company in Zimbabwe. we navigate the most scenic and challenging river in Africa. Our guides are certified professionals with over 10 years experience, ensuring an unforgettable experience for all who are brave enough.
